Data is stored in this browser's local storage on this device only. Tap the share icon → "Add to Home Screen" to open it like a native app.

Tap "PDF → Image" on any bill to pick a downloaded invoice PDF — the first page converts to a PNG and downloads automatically, ready to attach to your FBTO claim. Requires an internet connection (loads a PDF-rendering library).

Tap "⬇️ Export backup" any time to save all your data as a JSON file — keep it in Files as a safety net. If this app's local storage ever gets wiped (cache clear, new device, etc.), tap "⬆️ Import backup" and pick that JSON file to restore everything. The app also tries to auto-load a "bills-data.json" file sitting next to it, but that only works if the page is hosted on a real web server — when opened as a local file on your iPad (the normal case), it's skipped automatically and local storage is used instead.

"FBTO reimbursed" is the cumulative reimbursement total reconciled from your bank statement (8 payments, 2025-11-27 to 2026-06-08). "Net out-of-pocket" = total paid − total reimbursed, an overall estimate — not every bill has been individually reconciled (some claims may be denied, some count toward the annual deductible / eigen risico).
